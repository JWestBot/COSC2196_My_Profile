Behaviours

Different personality types work differently with each other in teams, being aware and conscious of other personality and learning types as well as your own will give you the best chance of succeeding in a team situation. However having complimentary personality and learning types together in teams, not all the same types of people, would be the best way of ensuring success.

Forming A Team

On any project different tasks can be suited to people with different skills and personality traits and types of learning. When forming a team, I will be trying to find team members that compliment my own traits, skills and learning type. For instance, I’m not someone that would be the first person to stand up in front of a group of people and present my findings from these tests. I would more likely be someone who observes others first and when I am comfortable and have a better understanding of the people in the room and I feel safe, I will then be able to present. So I will be drawn to more balanced, introverted and reserved people rather than extroverted people as I will have more confidence that I will have space and time to contribute and use my experience to get the best out of myself and the wider team.
